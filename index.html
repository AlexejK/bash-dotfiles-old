Welcome to Dot Files

This is where you can find a setup of dot files that will make your life a little easier

What is Dot Files?

http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dot-file

Why would I even want this?

Mac OSX is a great OS. But sometimes you want it to be a little different. You wish for color highlights, easy to use commands, configurations of different programs etc.. And you do it all over again each time you restore your Mac or buy a new one.

With dot files hosted on Git, all you have to do is get a copy from GitHub, and all your configuration is back! (Well, most of it).
